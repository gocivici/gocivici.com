Faster Than the Blink of an Eye

It has been 2 months since I published my project Star Track on Instructables. Publishing one of my projects online was a big step for me, and the main reason for that was the feedback I got. While expecting no feedback at all, I got mostly feedback like this from various platforms: You can use a small NEMA 17 steppers. With 200steps/rev and the micro stepping features of the drivers, you can get a reasonably high precision.
